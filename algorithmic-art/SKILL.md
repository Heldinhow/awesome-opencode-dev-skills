## Techniques

1. **Seeded Randomness** - Reproducible results
2. **Flow Fields** - Perlin noise vectors
3. **Particle Systems** - Physics-based particles
4. **L-Systems** - Fractal patterns

## Best Practices

- Use seeded random for consistency
- Optimize for performance
- Export as GIF/MP4
- Consider color palettes
